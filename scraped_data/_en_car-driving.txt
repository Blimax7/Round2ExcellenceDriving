Car Driving License FAQs
What Is An LMV Driving License?
An LMV (Light Motor Vehicle) driving license is issued in Dubai by the RTA (Road and Transport Authority) of Dubai. This license allows you to drive a light motor vehicle (not including trucks or any other heavy motor vehicles). Such as cars, SUVs, and small vans, having a maximum weight of up to 2.5 tons. Such a license is required for any person who wishes to drive privately or professionally in the UAE.

It is issued to all who aim to drive LMVs in the UAE.

How Long Is A UAE Driving License Valid?
The validity of a UAE driving license depends on the age of the license holder. If the person is less than 21 years old, the license is valid for one year and if the license holder is 21 years old or above the license is valid for two years. So, once the validity term is over, you have to renew your license.
